Django Todo App

This Django app allows you to manage your tasks easily and efficiently. With it, you can create new tasks, assign a title, description, and set a due date. Additionally, you will have the option to edit any task at any time, whether it's to update its information or make changes to its details. If a task is no longer needed or has been completed, you can easily delete it. You can also mark tasks as completed, helping you keep a clear record of your achievements. In summary, this app provides you with all the tools you need to keep your tasks organized and ensure that nothing is left undone.
